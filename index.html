My name is Rees Draminski, and I am an undergraduate currently studying Philosophy and Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). I plan to graduate in May 2022.

My interests, in no particular order, include, but are not limited to:

  • Philosophy of Technology
  • Computer Science Education (CSEd)
  • Cross-Platform Webapp Development
  • Gamification

I currently serve as the Director of Technology for TeCanal, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to providing STEM education and technology resources to underserved communities. I develop curriculum and technological tools to teach Computer Science in underresourced schools, most of the time with limited Internet connections.

In the past, I was a part of the Inaugural Microsoft Council for Digital Good which was comprised of 15 students from around the U.S. We met around and discussed digital civility, social media, privacy, and other Internet-related topics. With this organization, I was able to speak at a UNICEF-Microsoft joint hosted panel called Empowering Children in a Digital Age.
