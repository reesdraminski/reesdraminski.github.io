Recipes

Cooking is somewhat of a hobby to me (other than me having to cook to feed myself). I enjoy experimenting in the kitchen and expanding on other people's ideas/recipes. I love the idea of fusion food, and try to make it often. As some of mixed race heritage, I think that there is something so beautiful (and tasty) about marrying the flavors of different cultures together into one dish. All the recipes featured on this page are conceived of, cooked by, and written up by me. If there is an inspiration, I will always try to credit the source.
